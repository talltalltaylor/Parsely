Parsely

Browser Extension that parses HTML cooking pages into formatted, abridged recipes. Usage:
  • Click the Parsely logo in the browser to open the menu.
  • The background of Parsely is intitially white. When you click the button to parse or un-parse, the background will change color.
  • The Parsely extension is currently working on the following websites:
    • averiecooks.com
    • belleofthekitchen.com
    • cafedelites.com
    • cookieandkate.com
    • cookingclassy.com
    • damndelicious.net
    • divascancook.com
    • gimmesomeoven.com
    • loveandlemons.com
    • minimalistbaker.com
    • mysuburbankitchen.com
    • recipetineats.com
    • shewearsmanyhats.com
    • simplyrecipes.com
    • skinnytaste.com
    • spendwithpennies.com
    • spicysouthernkitchen.com
    • sugarspunrun.com
    • tastesbetterfromscratch.com
    • thestayathomechef.com
    • wellplated.com
  • Clicking the settings icon will open the optional settings. Click it again to close settings.
  • Clicking the help button will open this page in a seperate tab.
  • To close Parsely, click the extension icon in the browser.
