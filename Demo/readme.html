Parsely

Browser Extension that parses HTML cooking pages into formatted, abridged recipes. Usage:
  • Click the Parsely logo while viewing one of the sites in our domain list to parse a recipe, if one is present.
  • The background of Parsely is intitially white. When the parse functionality is on, the background turns green.
  • Click the Parsely logo again to turn off.
  • The Parsely extension is curruntly working on the following websites:
  • Clicking the settings icon will open the optional settings. Click it again to close settings.
  • Clicking the help button will open this page in a seperate tab.
  • To close Parsely, click the extension icon in the browser.
