Parsely Browser Extension

Browser Extension that parses HTML cooking pages into formatted, abridged recipes. Usage:
  • Clicking the icon in the browser brings up the menu with three options: Parse, Settings and Help.
  • Click the parsely logo (currently non-operational) while viewing one of the sites in our domain list to parse a recipe, if one is present.
  • Click the settings icon in the middle to open up the optional settings, then click it again to close the menu.
  • Currently, the extension is not in working order, but we're working on that!
